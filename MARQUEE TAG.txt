<P>---> The &LT MARQUEE TAG &GT is used for scrolling piece of text or image displayed either top to bottom (or) viceversa right to left (or) viceversa.</P>

    <p><li><h2><b>Width</b></h2>---> provides the width 0r breadtg of marquee.
    <p>--->EX: &LT marquee width="10" or width="10%" &GT</p></li></p>
